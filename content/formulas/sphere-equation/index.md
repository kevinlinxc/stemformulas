The equation of a sphere is:

$$ R^2 = (x-x_0)^2 + (y-y_0)^2 + (z - z_0)^2 $$

Where

* \\( \small R \\) is the radius of the sphere,
* \\( \small (x_0, y_0, z_0) \\) is the center of the sphere, and
* \\( \small (x, y, z) \\) is a point on the sphere.
