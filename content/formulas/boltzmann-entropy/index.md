The Boltzmann Entropy formula is:

$$ S = k_B \ln \Omega $$

Where

* \\( \small S \\) is the Boltzmann Entropy,
* \\( \small k_B \\) is Boltzmann's constant, and
* \\( \small \Omega \\) is the number of microstates.
