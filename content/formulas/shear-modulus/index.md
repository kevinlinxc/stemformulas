The modulus of rigidity or shear modulus formula is:

$$ G = \frac{\tau}{\gamma} $$

Where

* \\( \small G \\) is the modulus of rigidity or shear modulus,
* \\( \small\tau \\) is the shear stress, and
* \\( \small\gamma \\) is the shear strain.
