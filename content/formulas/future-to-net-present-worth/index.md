For a sum of money \\(P\\) invested at a compounding interest rate \\(i\\) for \\(n\\) periods, the future value \\(F\\) is given by:

{{< katex >}}

F = P(1 + i)^n

Where

* \\(P\\) is the present value,
* \\(i\\) is the interest rate, and
* \\(n\\) is the number of periods
