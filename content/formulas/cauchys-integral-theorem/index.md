{{< katex >}}
$$ \oint_C f(z)dz = 0 $$

Where
* \\(f(z)\\) is a [holomorphic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Holomorphic_function) complex function, and
* \\(C\\) is a [simple closed curve](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simple_closed_curve) in the complex plane.
