The formula for the convolution of two functions \\(\small f\\) and \\(\small g\\) is:

{{< katex >}}
$$ (f*g)(t)=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(\tau)g(t-\tau)d\tau$$

Where
* \\(\tau\\) is a dummy variable that is used in the integral only.
