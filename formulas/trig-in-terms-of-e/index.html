Trig functions in terms of e

math trigonometry e complex analysis imaginary numbers
Added on Nov 28 2022
Table of Contents

$$\small sin(x) = \frac{e^{ix} - e^{-ix}}{2i}, cos(x) = \frac{e^{ix} + e^{-ix}}{2}, tan(x) = \frac{e^{ix} - e^{-ix}}{i(e^{ix} + e^{-ix})}$$

Where

  • \(e\) is Euler’s number ~= 2.71828, and
  • \(i\) is the imaginary unit, \(\sqrt{-1}\)
